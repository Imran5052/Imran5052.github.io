4. GDPR & UK Privacy Rights (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the UK, you have specific rights:

  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can change your ad preferences at any time via the "Privacy Settings" in our app.
  • Right to Access: You can request information about how your data is processed by our ad partners.
  • Transparency: We only serve personalized ads in the EEA if you have given explicit consent through our GDPR popup.

5. US State Privacy Rights (CPRA/CCPA)

Residents of California and other US states have the right to:

  • Opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Request deletion of data collected by third-party SDKs.
  • To exercise these rights, please use the "Do Not Sell My Info" link provided in the app's settings menu.

6. Children's Privacy

Live+ Football Tv, M3u Checker does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our advertising partners are instructed to comply with COPPA regulations when serving ads.
