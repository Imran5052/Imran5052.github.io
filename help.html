Follow the visual steps to unlock live streaming instantly.

1
Select Your Channel

Browse through our extensive list of sports channels. Tap on your favorite channel icon to begin the process.

2
Generate Streaming Link

The 'Link Generator' popup will appear. Click on the orange GENERATE LINK button to fetch the secure streaming code.

4
Enjoy Live Sports

Success! Your stream is now active. You can now enjoy high-quality sports events. Don't forget to Share the app with friends!
